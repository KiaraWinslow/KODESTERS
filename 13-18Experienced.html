Kode With Klossy

Kode with Klossy offers programs for both beginners and coders with experience. They educate girls between 13-18 who want to learn how to code- for free! Click the link to find a program near you!

Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code is an organization aiming to close the gender gap in tech by 2027. They host summer courses, for expirienced and unexpirienced and have connections in schools across the country.

42 Hack Highschool

42 offers various programs throughout the year for high school students. Their goal is to help teens learn coding and digital skills for the 21st century. Their main program, HackHighSchool, runs from September to May. Their summer camp will run for six weeks with five levels of classes to choose from.
